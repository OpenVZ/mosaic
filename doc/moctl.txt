MOsaic ConTroL tool.

Syntaxis is

   moctl <object>
          ` mosaic   <action>
	              ` list
		        show    <name>
			add     <name> <elements>
			del     <name>
			change  <name> <elements>
			mount   <name> <path> [<options>]
			umount  <name> [<path>]

          ` tessera  <action>
	              ` list
		        show    <name>
			add     <name> <type> <options>
			del     <name>
			grow    <name> <age>[:<base-age>]
			mount   <name> <path> [<options>]
			umount  <name> [<path>]

For mosaic <elements> are "<tessera-name>:<age>:<location>[:<options>]".
The <options> can be "options=<mount-options>"
